Bingjie Tang

I am a PhD student at the Computer Science Department, part of the Viterbi School of Engineering at University of Southern California, where I work on robot manipulation. My PhD advisor is Gaurav S. Sukhatme.

I have an MS in Computer Science from Brown University, where I was a research assistant for George Konidaris and Stefanie Tellex. I am also a teaching assistant for Stefanie Tellex (CS1951R) & Eugene Charniak (CS1460). I received a bachelor degree in CS from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and I did my final year project at Shanghai Jiao Tong University with Hai Zhao.
